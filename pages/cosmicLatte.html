Cosmic Latte is a color theme I designed for my own use. It is inspired by and designed around the average color of the universe.

#1c1415
is used for elements that recess, or are "behind" the basic backgound.

#251c1c
is the backgound color.

#453a38
is used for subtle, less important elements.

#655956
is used for subtle elements that need more prominence, like comments.

#9a8d88
is used for normal text to make other text look brighter.
#fff8e7
is the foreground color.

#df6576
is red.
#fbb494
is orange.
#f9ff8f
is yellow.
#92fb8a
is lime.
#59c980
is green.
#b0f8f7
is cyan.
#9ac8fb
is light blue.
#6979e7
is blue.
#b768df
is purple.
#efa4ed
is pink.

The whole idea of making this page comes from the website for the Nord theme, which I think is hilariously over-designed. I don't thinka a set of 16 colors needs documentation, but a simple page listing and explaining them might be nice.
